About Me

I am a 4 year old mini cockapoo. I met my mom when I was 8 weeks old. I am the only pet in my house. When I go for walks, I meet dogs and play with them. Then we return home.
My favorite treat is a chicken stick. I enjoy collecting soft, stuffed toys. I like to sleep with mommy's pillows and wake up whenever I would like. I do not like routines.
